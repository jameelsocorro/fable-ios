# Version Changelog

Copy-paste source for App Store Connect `What's New in This Version`.

- Last updated: 2026-05-05
- Current version: 1.0
- App state: pre-launch

Maintenance rule:

- Add a new version section to the top when bumping the version.
- Each entry should be ready to paste directly into App Store Connect.
- Write from the user's perspective, not the developer's.
- Do not mention unreleased features, future roadmap, internal implementation details, or unvalidated results.
- Keep each entry under 4000 characters, which is the App Store Connect limit.

## Version 1.0

```text
What's new in Orion v1.0

Initial release of Orion, a daily visibility companion for founders, creators, indie hackers, and solopreneurs who want to build an online presence while they build.

- Guide-led onboarding with Oreo and platform selection
- Platform-specific quests for Instagram, TikTok, Threads, YouTube, Facebook, LinkedIn, X, Bluesky, and Reddit
- Today tab with filtered quest cards, plus-button completion, undo, animated fill, and haptics
- Streaks tab with total completions, current streak, monthly completion rate, a 12-month activity board, and per-quest seven-day strips
- Settings for platform editing, appearance, notification permission, and app version
- Local progress tracking with no account or social login required
```
