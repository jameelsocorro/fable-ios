### Recommended App Review Notes

```text
No account or sign-in is required.

On first launch, Orion shows a short onboarding flow. Please complete onboarding to enter the main app.

Version 1.0 review path:
1. Tap "Start" on the welcome screen.
2. Select one or more platforms from the orb picker. Supported platforms are Instagram, TikTok, Threads, YouTube, Facebook, LinkedIn, X, Bluesky, and Reddit.
3. Tap "Continue" after selecting at least one platform.
4. Orion opens to Today with quest cards filtered to the selected platforms.
5. In Today, tap the plus button on a quest card to mark it complete. The card fills with the platform color and then shows undo.
6. Tap undo to verify that the completion can be removed.
7. Use the recent-day strip to inspect recent dates. The current build allows completions to be recorded against a selected recent day.
8. Open Streaks to review total completions, current streak, current-month completion rate, the 12-month activity board, and per-quest seven-day strips.
9. Open Settings to edit selected platforms, change appearance, request notification permission, and view the app version.

Orion does not connect to social accounts, publish posts, verify posts, collect external credentials, or require a backend. Quest completion is a manual user action after the user performs the posting action outside Orion.

All profile, platform, and completion data in this build is stored locally on device with SwiftData.
```
